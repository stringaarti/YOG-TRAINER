Importants Of Yog

Community Class

For yog teachers who are interested in teaching in community settings, we recommend scheduling a visit to the location prior to their first class. If possible, meeting with the staff and program participants to introduce yourself and answer any questions about yoga/your class is also a great idea.

Power Yog

power yog is incredubibly good exercise as it is aerobic and rigorous.Through quick movement and ling holds ,power yoga is designed to increase your heart rete,strengthen your muscles and bulid endurance, as well as improve your flexibility and balance.

Foundation yog

Classes at Foundation Yog are designed to help students achieve their wellness goals. Whether you are looking to increase flexibility or strength, internal peace or healing; our teachers are meticulously trained and deeply passionate about helping you get the most from your yoga practice. .
